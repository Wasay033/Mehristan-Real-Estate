About Mehristan Real Estate

Mehristan Real Estate was the brainchild of Pakistani brothers, Abdul Sattar and Ghulam Rasool . Their mission was to make Pakistani real estate accessible and convenient for everyone. Together, they proceeded to lead Mehristan Real Estate from a small start-up to the premier real estate entity of Pakistan. Mehristan Real Estate started its funding first round in 2018 which constituted of several angel investors and substantial investment from venture capital firms. A further two rounds of major investment were closed in late 2020 and early 2019, bringing in a total of $5 million.
